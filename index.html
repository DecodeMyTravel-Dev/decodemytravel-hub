Baggage Considerations: 2026 Manual

1. The Standard for the "Big Three" (AA, Delta, United)

In 2026, the "Standard" has moved to Volumetric Compliance. If your bag doesn't slide into the automated metal sizer without a shove, it's a gate-check.

  • Personal Item: 17 x 10 x 9 inches. Must fit entirely under the seat.
  • Carry-On: 22 x 14 x 9 inches. Must include wheels and handles.
  • Checked Bag (Economy): 50 lbs (23 kg) limit. Fees average $45–$50.

2. Regional Airlines

International regional carriers often cap carry-ons at 15–22 lbs (7–10 kg). Overages are charged per kilo at the counter.

The Global Tipping Manual (2026)

1. The "Automatic" Trap: 2026 Auto-Gratuity covers room/dining staff, but NOT shore guides.

2. Shore Excursion Etiquette: Drivers: $2-$5 per person. Specialist Guides: $10-$20. Private Drivers: 10-15%.

3. Port Intelligence: Look for "Servicio Incluido" or "Propina" on Mexican bills before adding a tip.
