Airport & Port Exchange Traps

1. The "Convenience" Tax at Arrivals

Exchange kiosks at airport arrivals (Travelex, Global Blue) are the most expensive places on earth to get cash. You will often lose **12-15%** of your value through a combination of bad rates and "zero commission" lies.

2. Airport & Cruise Port ATMs

ATMs located inside the terminal or right at the cruise pier are often "Private" (like Euronet). These machines have two ways of taking your money:

  • High flat-rate usage fees ($5-$10 per pull).
  • "Suggested" conversion rates that are 10% worse than the bank rate.

3. The 2026 Strategy

Carry $100 in local cash for emergencies, but use a credit card with No Foreign Transaction Fees for 90% of purchases. If you MUST use an ATM, walk two blocks away from the airport or port to find a "Big Brand" local bank machine.
