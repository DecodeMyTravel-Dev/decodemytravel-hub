Baggage Considerations: The 2026 Manual

1. The "Big Three" (AA, Delta, United): Standard 2026 limit is Volumetric Compliance. If it doesn't fit the sizer, it's a gate-check.

  • Personal Item: 17x10x9in (Under seat).
  • Carry-On: 22x14x9in (Incl. wheels/handles).
  • Checked: 50lbs (23kg) Economy / 70lbs (32kg) First.

2. Regional Airlines: Extreme weight sensitivity. Often capped at 10kg carry-on. Excess charged per kilo at counter.

3. Surcharges: $100-$200 for overweight bags. Fuel surcharges may apply regionally.

Tipping/Gratuities Guide

Cruise Tips: Daily rates cover room/dining but NOT shore guides.

Excursions: $2-$5 for bus drivers; $10-$20 for specialist guides. Private drivers expect 10-15%.

Currency: USD is king in Caribbean/Mexico. Local currency preferred in Europe/Asia.
