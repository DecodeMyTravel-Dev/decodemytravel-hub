Baggage Considerations: The 2026 Manual

1. Major Carriers (AA, Delta, United)

Standard for 2026: Volumetric Compliance. If it doesn't fit the sizer, it's a gate-check.

  • PERSONAL ITEM: 17x10x9in (Must fit under seat).
  • CARRY-ON: 22x14x9in (Includes wheels/handles).
  • CHECKED (ECONOMY): 50 lbs (23 kg). Fees avg $45-$50.
  • CHECKED (FIRST): 70 lbs (32 kg).

2. Regional Airlines

Extreme sensitivity. Carry-ons often capped at 15–22 lbs (7–10 kg). Many allow only 10kg for Economy.

3. Surcharges & Fuel

Overweight fees: $100–$200 for 51-70 lbs. Regional carriers may apply extra $10–$25 security/fuel fees at counter.
