Baggage Considerations: The 2026 Manual

1. Standard for "Big Three" (AA, Delta, United): Standard for 2026 is Volumetric Compliance. If it doesn't fit the sizer, it's a gate-check.

  • Personal Item: 17x10x9in. Must fit under seat.
  • Carry-On: 22x14x9in. Must include wheels and handles.
  • Checked Bag: 50 lbs (23 kg) Economy / 70 lbs (32 kg) First.

2. Regional Carriers: Extreme weight sensitivity. Often capped at 10kg carry-on. Excess charged per kilo.

The Global Tipping Manual (2026)

1. The "Automatic" Trap: 2026 Auto-Gratuity ($18-$20/day) covers room/dining staff, but NOT shore excursion guides.

2. Shore Excursion Etiquette: Drivers: $2-$5 per person. Specialist Guides: $10-$20. Private Drivers: 10-15%.

3. Port Intelligence: USD is King in Mexico/Bahamas. Look for "Servicio Incluido" on restaurant bills before tipping extra.
