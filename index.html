Baggage Considerations: 2026 Intelligence

1. The Major Three (AA, Delta, United): 2026 has shifted to "Volumetric Compliance." If it doesn't fit the metal sizer without a shove, it's a gate-check.

  • Personal Item: 17x10x9in (Must fit under seat).
  • Carry-On: 22x14x9in (Includes wheels/handles).
  • Checked (Economy): 50 lbs (23 kg). Fees average $45-$50.
  • First Class: 70 lbs (32 kg) limit.

2. Regional Airlines: Extreme weight sensitivity. Regional carriers often cap carry-ons at 15-22 lbs (7-10 kg). Anything over is charged per kilo at the counter.

3. Fuel Factors: Overweight Economy bags trigger flat $100-$200 surcharges. Regional carriers may apply additional $10-$25 security/fuel fees at the counter.
