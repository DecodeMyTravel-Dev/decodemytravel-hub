Baggage Considerations: 2026 Manual

1. The Standard for the "Big Three" (American, Delta, United)
Standard has moved to Volumetric Compliance. If it doesn't slide into the sizer without a shove, it's a gate-check.

  • Personal Item: 17x10x9in. Must fit under the seat. (Basic Economy restricted to ONLY this).
  • Carry-On: 22x14x9in. Must include wheels and handles.
  • Checked Bag (Economy): 50 lbs (23 kg). Fees average $45-$50.
  • Checked Bag (First/Business): 70 lbs (32 kg).

2. Regional Airlines
Carry-on caps of 15–22 lbs (7–10 kg). Overages charged per kilo.

3. Surcharges & Fuel Factors
Fuel Surcharge (YQ/YR codes) and $10-$25 "Security & Fuel Handling" fees common on regional hops.

The Global Tipping Manual (2026)

1. The "Automatic" Trap: 2026 Auto-Gratuity ($18-$20/day) covers room/dining staff, but NOT shore excursion guides.

2. Shore Excursion Etiquette: Drivers: $2-$5 per person. Specialist Guides: $10-$20. Private Drivers: 10-15% of cost.

3. Port Intelligence: Look for "Servicio Incluido" or "Propina" on Mexican bills. USD $1 bills are king in Caribbean/Bahamas ports.
