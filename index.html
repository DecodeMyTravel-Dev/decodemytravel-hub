Baggage Considerations: The 2026 Manual

1. Standard for Major Carriers (AA, Delta, United)
In 2026, the standard is Volumetric Compliance. If it doesn't fit the automated sizer, it's a gate-check.

  • Personal Item: 17 x 10 x 9 inches. Must fit under seat.
  • Carry-On: 22 x 14 x 9 inches. Includes wheels and handles.
  • Checked (Economy): 50 lbs (23 kg). Fees average $45-$50.
  • Checked (First/Business): 70 lbs (32 kg).
