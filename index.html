Baggage Considerations: The 2026 Manual

1. Major Carriers (AA, Delta, United)

  • Personal Item: 17x10x9in (Must fit under seat).
  • Carry-On: 22x14x9in (Includes wheels/handles).
  • Checked (Economy): 50 lbs (23 kg). Fees average $45-$50.
  • Checked (First/Business): 70 lbs (32 kg).

2. Regional Airlines

Traveling outside major hubs requires extreme caution. Carry-ons often capped at 15–22 lbs (7–10 kg). Many allow only 10kg for Economy.

3. Surcharges & Fuel

Overweight fees: Crossing the 50lb mark in Economy triggers surcharges of $100–$200.
