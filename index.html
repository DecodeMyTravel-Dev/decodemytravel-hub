Baggage Considerations (2026) ▼

1. MAJOR CARRIERS (AA, DELTA, UNITED)

Standard: Strictly Volumetric. Personal Item (17x10x9in) must fit under seat. Carry-on (22x14x9in) must include wheels/handles. Checked (Economy) capped at 50 lbs (23 kg) with fees averaging $45-50.

First/Business: 70 lbs (32 kg) limit. Most premium tickets include 2-3 bags free.

2. REGIONAL AIRLINES

Extreme weight sensitivity is common. Regional carriers often cap carry-ons at 15–22 lbs (7–10 kg). Excess is moved to hold at the jet bridge.

3. SURCHARGES & FUEL

Overweight Economy bags (51-70 lbs) trigger flat $100–$200 surcharges. Note the "Variable Fuel Surcharge" (YQ/YR codes); regional carriers may apply extra $10–$25 fees at the counter.

Estimates provided for planning. Consult carrier website 72h before flying for latest DOT/IATA adjustments.
